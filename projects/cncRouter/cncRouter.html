CNC Router Development

I recently purchased a 3D printer with the intent to learn more about robotics and mechanical assemblies. I was thrilled by my $160 base-model Ender 3's accuracy and detail, but was seriously disappointed at the amount of time it took to print even the smallest part. Because I knew I wanted to work on larger projects in the future, I decided to use my new 3D printer to create a CNC router so I could cut plywood and other such materials rapidly.

Instead of simply downloading and printing freely available designs for simple three-axis routers from other creators, I set out to design my own from scratch. This is the current design for my CNC router for which I have designed all of the circuitry. I also programmed the controller and slicer to be able to take images and create router designs easily.

This project is very much ongoing, and I am hoping to show some of the results here soon!
