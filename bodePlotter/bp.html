Logic Simulator
Instructions:
This is a logarithmic plotter!
Input any equation with variable x, and it will create the bode plot for the function.
In general, you should input transfer functions H(s)=Vout/Vin

Valid symbols are:
Numbers & Decimal points
'x' is the variable
'(' & ')' for parentheses
'^' as the power operator
'*' & '/' for multiplication and division
'+' & '-' for addition and subtraction

Notes:
White spaces are ignored.
This (should) follow PEMDAS.
For exponents, put the exponent inside of '(' ')'
Capital letters are NOT allowed EVER. (breaks internal functionality)
